About Me

Hello, I'm Amogh Gajare. I'm a recent graduate from University of Mumbai. I aspire to be a professor in the future because teaching is something I absolutely love. May it be in the form of mentoring my juniors, conducting workshops on electronics for them, or even explaining a concept my peer is having a hard time understanding, I find joy in doing it! What I love the most about the teaching is that it is in itself a learning experience. What I fear the most is my knowledge becoming stagnant and teaching helps me counter that fear as it constantly provides one with different perspectives and propels one towards constant improvement. This nature of teaching process really helps in research as well, because research is all about not letting scientific knowledge become stagnant, constantly improving it.
